Patch Fitting Doors

Patch fitting doors are modern, frameless glass doors that offer a sleek, minimalistic appearance, making them ideal for commercial spaces, showrooms, malls, and offices. These doors use high-quality stainless steel patch fittings to hold toughened glass panels without traditional frames, ensuring both elegance and stability.

Automated sliding doors offer a touch-free, intelligent entrance solution, commonly used in airports, malls, hospitals, hotels, and corporate offices. Powered by sensors and automation systems, these doors open and close smoothly based on motion detection or access control systems.

Sliding Folding Doors

Sliding folding doors, also known as bi-fold doors, offer a versatile and space-saving solution for wide openings. These doors combine sliding and folding mechanisms to allow maximum opening width, ideal for showrooms, patios, balconies, commercial storefronts, and conference rooms.
