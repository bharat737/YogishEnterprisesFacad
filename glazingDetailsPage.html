Spider Glazing

Spider glazing is a frameless glass system that uses stainless steel "spider" fittings to support large glass panels, providing a sleek and modern appearance with maximum visibility. Ideal for commercial buildings, malls, and atriums, spider glazing creates a seamless glass façade while maintaining structural strength.

At Yogisha Enterprises, we offer advanced spider glazing solutions with precision engineering for point-fixed, minimalistic aesthetics and high wind-load resistance.

Cap Curtain Wall Glazing

Cap curtain wall glazing is a type of stick-built glazing system where vertical and horizontal aluminium members are used to fix the glass panels, and the joints are covered with pressure plates and external caps. This traditional curtain wall offers strong structural performance, clean lines, and allows for thermal insulation and water tightness.

Semi Unitized Glazing

Semi-unitized glazing is a modern façade solution that balances the benefits of unitized and stick glazing systems. In this system, vertical mullions are installed on-site, while glass units with pre-assembled frames are fabricated in the factory.This method ensures faster installation, improved alignment, and higher quality control compared to traditional stick glazing.

Yogisha Enterprises specializes in semi-unitized glazing systems ideal for buildings requiring a blend of precision, speed, and performance.

Fixed Glass Partition

Fixed glass partitions are non-movable glass panels used to divide interior spaces without interrupting natural light flow. These partitions provide a modern, open feel while offering privacy and noise reduction.Suitable for office interiors, showrooms, and residential settings.

Yogisha Enterprises designs fixed glass partitions with toughened or laminated safety glass and a range of frame or frameless options.

Aluminium Glass Partition

Aluminium glass partitions combine aluminium framing with glass panels to create durable, lightweight, and stylish interior divisions. These are widely used in commercial interiors, offering flexibility in design and easy maintenance.

Yogisha Enterprises offers customized aluminium glass partitions in various finishes, including powder-coated or anodized aluminium, ensuring both aesthetic appeal and long-term performance.
